Fermentation can ...
  • make food more nutritious, even disease-fighting
  • preserve food easily
  • make food taste better.
  • make new kinds of food. Many common foods were originally fermented.

The process of fermentation

Fermentation means micro-organisms are consuming or transforming one thing into another. To ferment, you need three things:
  • Something to ferment. Milk, cabbage, flour, meat, others.
  • A micro-organism. These come from the air, or from a starter, which might simply be a bit of leftover from the last batch.
  • The proper environment. This means the proper temperature, salinity, pH, oxygen availability, etc.

Sourdough

Sourdough

Sourdough is easy. Mix flour with water. Wait.
  • Use the same kind of flour all the time
  • Feed it regularly
  • Use non-chlorinated water where possible
  • Keep it warm
